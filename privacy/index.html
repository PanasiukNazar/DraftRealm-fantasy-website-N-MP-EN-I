Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to our fantasy sport platform. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information in relation to our fantasy sport services. Accessing our website and participating in our fantasy leagues is possible without submitting personal data, unless you choose to register or use our features.

2. Responsible Party

The party responsible for data processing on this platform is our company. If you have any inquiries about how your data is handled on our fantasy sport site, please contact us directly.

3. Collection and Processing of Personal Data

We collect and process only the data necessary to offer an optimized and engaging fantasy sport experience. Personal data includes information that can identify you directly or indirectly.

4. Data We Collect

a. Server Logs: When you visit our site, certain technical data is automatically logged, including:

  • Your IP address
  • Time and date of access
  • Requested content and file names
  • Referral source (e.g., previous site or link)
  • Browser type and version, and system information

b. Contact Form: If you contact us using the online form, your information (name, email, message) is saved to respond to your fantasy sport inquiries.

c. Registration: When creating an account to join our fantasy leagues or Earn Gift Code rewards, we collect:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Chosen password

5. Use of Your Data

We use your data exclusively for the following fantasy sport-related purposes:

  • To provide access to our fantasy leagues and services
  • To communicate with you about league updates and contests
  • To process Earn Gift Code promotions and redemptions
  • To improve user experience and performance of the platform

8. Your Rights

You are entitled to the following rights regarding your fantasy sport account data:

  • Request information about the data we hold

9. Duration of Data Storage

We retain your data only as long as needed to support your use of our fantasy sport platform, fulfill your Earn Gift Code claims, or comply with legal obligations.

10. Security

Our platform uses advanced security practices to protect your data from unauthorized access, misuse, or loss. These safeguards are updated regularly to stay aligned with evolving standards.

11. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may revise this privacy policy to reflect changes to our fantasy sport services or legal updates. Any modifications will be posted on this page, and continued use of the site implies your agreement with the updated policy.

12. Contact

For any privacy-related concerns or inquiries about your fantasy sport account, feel free to reach out to us through our contact page. We're here to help ensure your data is secure and your experience is enjoyable.

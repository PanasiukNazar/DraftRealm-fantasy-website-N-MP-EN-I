Cookie Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to our fantasy sports platform. This cookie policy explains how we use cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience on our website. By continuing to use our website, you agree to our use of cookies. Please read this policy to understand how we use cookies and how you can manage them.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They allow the website to remember your actions and preferences (such as login details or language settings) over a period of time, so you don't have to re-enter them each time you visit.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

We use the following types of cookies on our fantasy sports platform:

  • Essential Cookies: These are necessary for the basic functionality of the website, such as logging in or accessing your fantasy team.
  • Performance Cookies: These cookies help us analyze how users interact with the website to improve its performance and user experience.
  • Functional Cookies: These cookies allow us to remember your preferences and customize your experience on the platform, such as setting your favorite fantasy leagues.
  • Advertising Cookies: These cookies are used to display personalized advertisements and promotions, such as "Earn Gift Code" offers for our fantasy sports games.

4. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • To remember your login details for a seamless fantasy sports experience.
  • To track your participation in fantasy leagues and display relevant game statistics.
  • To improve the functionality and performance of the website based on user behavior.
  • To personalize content and offer tailored promotions like "Earn Gift Code" for our fantasy games.

5. Managing Cookies

You have the right to manage your cookie preferences. You can set your browser to refuse all cookies or alert you when cookies are being sent. However, please note that some features of our platform may not function properly without cookies.

You can adjust your cookie settings through your browser settings. For more information, please consult the "Help" section of your browser or the official website of the browser you are using.

6. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services, such as analytics tools and advertising networks, that also use cookies to improve your experience on our platform. These third-party services may collect information about your usage of our website and deliver targeted ads based on your preferences. We do not have control over third-party cookies, so please refer to their respective privacy policies for more information.

7. Your Rights Regarding Cookies

You have the right to accept or decline cookies at any time. If you wish to withdraw your consent or disable cookies, you can do so through your browser settings, as mentioned earlier. However, some parts of the platform may not work as expected if cookies are disabled.

8. Changes to the Cookie Policy

We reserve the right to update or change this cookie policy at any time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "last modified" date. By continuing to use our platform after any updates, you agree to the revised policy.

9. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this cookie policy, please feel free to contact us. We are here to assist you with any inquiries related to our use of cookies.

This cookie policy is part of our commitment to transparency and user experience. We appreciate your trust in our platform and assure you that we are dedicated to respecting your privacy and preferences.
